Programs


As a Mobilus member, you have access to not only CrossFit but various specialised classes to augment your training needs.

MobEngine

The combination of spinning, rowing and skiing will create the most powerful cardiovascular response.

MobEngine’s programming can be individualize to challenge seasoned athletes to push past their limits, yet also providing beginners a progressive platform to find their engine again.

Think:

• Bikes, Rowers and Ski-Ergs
• Triathlons, intervals, mileage
• All levels are welcome
• No complicated exercises, just simply BikeRowSki

MobStrong

60 minutes of PURE Strength class, tailored towards increased power output. You will learn better form to lift heavier weights.

Think:

• A 1-hour strength-building class.
• Tailored towards increasing your brute strength and power output.
• Learn proper form and techniques that will enable you to lift heavier loads safely and efficiently.

Barbell strength training incorporating:

• The classic Power lifting movements (Squat, Bench Press, Dead lift)
• Accessory barbell movements to develop core and upper-body strength (Shoulder Press, Barbell Row)

Strongman training:

• Weighted sleds
• Tyre flips
• Odd Objects carry

MobLift

Meet me at the bar! Our in house barbell club where you learn and practise the sport of olympic weightlifting.

Think:

• Certified Olympic Weightlifting coaches working with you
• Structured strength cycle programming
• Lift weights, Slam weights!

MobYoga

Let’s stretch, properly.

Follow our guided yoga instructor to help you loosen up every tightness that limits your full range of motion. You don’t need another yoga membership, we got you covered.
