About Us

Mobilus Chinatown



Mobilus' second branch is situated at the heart of Chinatown, a stone throw away from Chinatown and Outram MRT. It
spans close to 4,800 sqft with structured fitness zones featuring a new and multi-functions training rig, one of the
largest in Singapore, for body weight and weightlifting work, main training area for regular open gyms and CrossFit
and a mobility and social zone to catch a breather before or after your workout.

All Mobilus facilities are equipped with shower facilities, lockers and stocked snack bars to get you in and out proper.
